Milkshake Recipe 

    
Ingredients:

        

    
    
    

    2 cups vanilla ice cream        

    ½ cup whole milk

    ¼ cup powdered chocolate drink mix

    1 teaspoon powdered egg whites
            
            

    


Steps to prepare the recipe:

    

    


    step1:
    In a blender, combine the ice cream, milk, chocolate drink mix 
    and powdered egg whites. 

    step2:    
    Cover, and blend until smooth. 

    step3:
    You may need to stop and stir once or twice to get it evenly mixed. The 
    shake will be very thick.
